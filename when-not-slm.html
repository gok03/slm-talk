When NOT to Use SLMs
Knowing the boundaries is what makes the architecture credible
Don't Use SLMs For
🧠
Multi-Hop Reasoning
GPT-4 drops to 41.6 EM on 3–4 hop chains — SLMs fare far worse. Chaining inferences across documents collapses accuracy exponentially.
MRKE Benchmark · arXiv:2402.11924
📏
Long Context (>32K tokens)
11 of 12 models drop below 50% of short-context perf at 32K. Middle sections: only 76–82% accuracy. Effective context is 10–20% of advertised window.
Context Rot · Chroma Research 2025
🌍
Broad World Knowledge
MMLU gap: 3B → 74 vs 70B+ → 90+ (16-pt gap). No model exceeds 70% on the FACTS factuality suite — SLMs average ~60%.
Qwen 2.5 Blog · FACTS Benchmark, DeepMind 2025
💻
Complex Code Generation
Gemma 3 4B: 71% HumanEval vs 90%+ frontier. Self-invoking, multi-function code diverges sharply. 4× VRAM needed for every 10% quality gain.
HumanEval Pro · ACL 2025 · arXiv:2507.03160
Production Failure Modes
🎭
Hallucination Amplification
Distilled SLMs: >80% hallucination rate vs ~50% for GPT-4o. Valid JSON, wrong content. CoT prompting cuts rate from 38% → 18%.
PMC Survey on LLM Hallucinations · 2025
📊
Confidence Blindness
Smaller models show the Dunning-Kruger effect — poorly performing models display markedly higher overconfidence. Structured choices yield 460% accuracy boost.
Mind the Confidence Gap · OpenReview 2025
📉
Format & Output Drift
Mixtral: 31% inconsistency in instruction adherence over long outputs. SLMs lose structure faster — distribution drift in hidden states compounds with length.
LLM Performance Drift Monitoring · 2025
🔄
Domain Shift Collapse
Fine-tuned SLMs are highly brittle to distribution shifts. Works on test data, fails in production. Catastrophic forgetting is the #1 deployment failure.
KILO · arXiv:2508.03571 · LLM Failure Taxonomy 2025
Mitigations
Constrained decoding — enforce schema
Verifier chain — 1B validates 3B
RAG — retrieval over recall
Cascade — escalate to LLM
Fine-tune — narrow domain
